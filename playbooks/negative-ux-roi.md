### How Negativ Delivers ROI
- Standardized `NEG-` codes + recovery actions → fewer vague “Something went wrong” moments
- Centralized messages + accessibility → consistent tone, lower frustration
- Negative journey maps → predictable escalation paths instead of chaos
- Research-ready kits → faster usability testing and iteration
