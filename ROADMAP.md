# Negativ Roadmap — 2026

**Building Negativ as a centralized, open, fundamentals-first knowledge base for negative UX.**

We fix the negative customer journey that design systems still ignore: errors, failures, edge cases, and recovery flows.  
This roadmap is **executable today** and is built exclusively around the real pain points of our core audience:

- **Product Managers** → “We can’t measure, prioritize, or communicate the business impact of negative UX.”
- **Developers** → “Error handling is still tribal knowledge; every team reinvents the wheel across platforms and frameworks.”
- **UX Researchers** → “Negative journeys are invisible — we lack reusable models, research protocols, and evidence-based recovery patterns.”
